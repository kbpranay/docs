# Integration Issues

If your bot isn't showing up or isn't responding on a specific channel, work through the checks below for that channel.

## Web / App (chatbot widget)

### The chat widget doesn't appear

1. Open **Go-Live** in AIDA and confirm the **Enable toggle** for Web/App is **ON**.
2. Re-copy the integration script from **Go-Live → Show script** — your page might have a stale snippet.
3. Open your site's browser console and look for script errors.
4. Confirm the chat window isn't blocked by a content-security policy or ad blocker.

### The bot loads but all replies are fallback

This usually means the Classifier is disabled. Open [Bot Settings → Classifier](/bot-settings/classifier) and turn it on. Until the first training cycle completes, you may continue to see fallbacks.

### My Multi-Build source isn't reflecting my change

- Click **Refresh** on the Go-Live page — builds have to finish before changes propagate.
- A source marked **In Progress** won't serve the new content yet. Wait for **Build Ready**.
- Re-publish the specific source using **Publish Builds** if the build appears stuck.

## Voice (AudioCodes)

### The call disconnects immediately

- Check that your AudioCodes configuration points to the URL generated under **Go-Live → Voice**.
- Confirm the **Voice toggle** on the Go-Live page is **ON**. When it's off, new incoming calls are disconnected with the offline message.
- If you just enabled Voice, allow a few minutes for the route to propagate.

### Ongoing calls continue but new calls fail

This is by design when the Voice toggle is disabled — ongoing calls complete, new calls receive the offline message. Re-enable the toggle to restore new-call handling.

## WhatsApp

### New WhatsApp messages aren't answered by the bot

1. Open **Go-Live → WhatsApp** and confirm the toggle is **ON**.
2. If the integration script was never generated, the toggle is disabled — click **Generate URL** first.
3. Existing sessions continue to work when the toggle is off; only new messages get the offline auto-reply.

### The offline message is wrong

Edit it directly on the Go-Live page. Click outside the field after editing and the new message auto-saves.

## Bot status says "indexing failed"

For DocBrain bots, this means the indexing job failed. Try:

1. Open the DocBrain page for your bot.
2. Re-index the source documents.
3. Check that your sources are accessible (URLs that require authentication won't index).
4. If the error persists, contact your AIDA administrator or EnterpriseBot support with the bot ID.

<Callout>
  Interactions that happen while a channel is disabled are **not** included in training data. They only show up in Total Conversations under Live Data and Analytics.
</Callout>

## Still stuck?

Try collecting these before contacting support:

- Bot ID (from Bot Settings → Bot Info)
- Channel(s) affected (Web, Voice, WhatsApp)
- A specific session ID where the issue happened (copy it from Training)
- Exact error message if one appears in the UI
