# Login & Access Issues

## Forgot Password

If you can't remember your password, use the self-service reset flow:

1. On the Login screen, click **[Forgot Password]**.
2. Enter your registered email address.
3. Check your inbox for a password reset email.
4. Click the link in the email and enter your new password.
5. You will receive a confirmation email once the password is successfully changed.

## Account Lockout

AIDA locks accounts after too many failed login attempts to prevent unauthorized access.

**Default lockout settings (applied globally across all teams):**

| Setting | Default value |
|---|---|
| Invalid attempts before lockout | **5** |
| Lockout duration | **30 minutes** |
| Minimum time between attempts | **60 seconds** |

After exceeding the attempt limit, your account is locked and a message is displayed. Wait for the lockout period (30 minutes by default) before trying again.

<Callout type="warning">
  If your account is still locked after the lockout period, contact your Team Owner or AIDA Admin.
</Callout>

Your Team Owner can adjust the lockout policy from **Team Settings → Security**.

## SSO Login Issues

AIDA supports Single Sign-On (SSO) via Gmail (Google), Microsoft, and Okta.

**If SSO login fails:**

1. Confirm that your organization has SSO enabled for AIDA.
2. Check that your SSO email matches your AIDA account email.
3. Try the email/password login as a fallback.
4. Contact your Team Owner to verify SSO configuration.

## Invitation & Joining a Team

If you received a team invitation but can't join:

1. Check your inbox for an email with a **Join Team** button.
2. Click **Join Team** — this redirects you to set your password.
3. If the invitation link has expired, ask your Team Owner to send a new invitation.

## Session Expiry

If your session expires while using AIDA:

- You will be redirected to the re-login page.
- After logging back in, you will be directed to the **same team** you were in before the session expired.

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

If 2FA is enabled for your team and you can't log in:

1. Enter your email and password as usual.
2. When prompted, click **[Setup]** to configure 2FA (first time only).
3. Enter the authentication code sent to your email.
4. Scan the QR code with your Authenticator app.
5. Click **[Go to Sign in]** to complete setup.

On subsequent logins, open your Authenticator app and enter the current code.

<Callout>
  2FA applies globally to your account — it is not team-specific.
</Callout>

## Can't Access Certain Pages

If you're unable to access a page or feature, your role may not have permission.

| Role | Restricted pages |
|---|---|
| AIDA User | User Rights, Create Bot, Team Settings |
| AIDA Dashboard | All pages except Live Data, Analytics, My Profile, Account, Timezone |
| AIDA Admin | Cannot activate/deactivate/delete users; cannot access Team Settings |

Contact your Team Owner to request a role change if needed.
