# Deep Links

Deep links let you share a direct URL to a specific intent, entity, or training session. Anyone on your team with the right role can click the link and jump straight to that resource — no navigating required.

## Where deep links are available

| Area | What the deep link opens | Actions available |
|---|---|---|
| **Intents** | A specific intent on the Intents page | Read, Update, Delete |
| **Entities** | A specific entity on the Entities page | Read, Update, Delete |
| **Sessions** | A specific training session on the Training page | Validate, Configure, Ignore |

## Copying a deep link

Each resource has a **copy icon** next to its name:

- **Intent:** On the Intents page, click the copy icon next to the intent name in the list.
- **Entity:** On the Entities page, click the copy icon next to the entity name.
- **Session:** On the Training page, click the copy icon on the session row you want to share.

The link is copied to your clipboard. Paste it into Slack, email, or anywhere you want to share it.

## Deep links for training sessions

When someone opens a session deep link, the **Session ID** input on the Training page is pre-filled with the ID. AIDA uses that to fetch and display just that specific session.

## Who can use deep links

<Callout>
  Deep links only work for users in the **same team** with access to that resource. Opening a link for a deleted resource, a different team's resource, or when you don't have the required role redirects you to the Bot List page with an error.
</Callout>

After opening a deep link, navigating anywhere else replaces the deep link with the default URL for the new page.

## When deep links don't work

- **Error and redirect to Bot List:** the resource was deleted, belongs to a different team, or your role doesn't allow access
- **"Session not found":** the session ID in the deep link doesn't match any training session (may have been purged after retention expired)
- **Link opens but nothing appears:** your role may not have read access to this resource type — ask your Team Owner to check your permissions
