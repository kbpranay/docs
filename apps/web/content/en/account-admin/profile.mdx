# Profile & Password

Your profile settings live under **Profile settings → Account** in the top-right dropdown.

## Updating your name

<Steps>

### Open Profile Settings

Click your profile icon in the top-right corner.

### Go to Account

Click the **Account** tab.

### Edit

Click **[Edit]** next to the My Profile field.

### Update and save

Change your first or last name, then click **[Save]**.

</Steps>

<Callout>
  Your email address is your account identifier and **cannot be changed**. You can also view — but not change — the roles assigned to you from this page.
</Callout>

## Changing your password

<Steps>

### Open Profile Settings

Click your profile icon → **Account** → **[Edit]**.

### Enter your passwords

Fill in all three fields:

- **Current password** — your existing password
- **New password** — the password you want to set
- **Confirm password** — re-enter the new password

### Save

Click **[Save]**. You'll receive a confirmation email.

</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
  Once you change your password, AIDA **logs you out of the current session**. Log back in with your new password.

  The **Save** button stays disabled until all three fields are filled.
</Callout>

## Timezone

By default, AIDA uses your preferred timezone for timestamps in logs, analytics, and scheduled features. You can change it from the Account page.

<Steps>

### Open Profile Settings → Account → Edit

Same starting point as the password change.

### Select a timezone

Click the **Timezone** dropdown and pick your preferred option.

### Save

Click **[Save]**.

</Steps>

<Callout>
  Logging out resets your timezone to **Asia/Calcutta**. You'll need to set it again after the next login.
</Callout>
