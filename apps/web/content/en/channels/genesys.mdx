# WhatsApp Channel

AIDA supports WhatsApp as a messaging channel, letting your bot handle customer conversations on WhatsApp directly.

## Setting Up WhatsApp Channel

<Steps>

### Open Go-Live

Click **Go Live** from the side menu bar.

### Go to the WhatsApp tab

Navigate to the **WhatsApp** section on the Go-Live page.

### Generate a URL

Click **[Generate URL]** to create your WhatsApp integration endpoint.

### Configure WhatsApp

Click **[How to configure]** for the detailed configuration steps. Follow the instructions to connect your WhatsApp Business account to the generated AIDA endpoint.

</Steps>

## Enable / Disable WhatsApp

The WhatsApp toggle on the Go-Live page lets you activate or deactivate the WhatsApp channel instantly.

| State | Behavior |
|---|---|
| **Toggle ON** | Bot handles all incoming WhatsApp messages normally |
| **Toggle OFF (script generated)** | Ongoing sessions continue; users can still send agent requests until the session auto-closes. New messages receive an **offline message** |
| **Toggle OFF (no script)** | Toggle is disabled; a notification is shown |

<Callout type="warning">
  The offline message is **editable** — click outside the field after editing and the message auto-saves.
</Callout>

<Callout>
  Interactions while WhatsApp is disabled are **not** included in training data. They are only counted under **Total Conversations** in Live Data and Analytics.
</Callout>

## Who Can Manage WhatsApp Channel

Only the following roles can enable/disable the WhatsApp integration:

- App Admin
- Team Owner
- Manager
- AIDA Admin
