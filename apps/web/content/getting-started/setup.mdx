# Login & Account Setup

## Logging In

To access AIDA, navigate to your AIDA instance URL and log in with your credentials.

<Steps>

### Enter your email address

On the Login screen, enter your **Email** and click **Next**.

You can also sign in using Single Sign-On (SSO):
- **Gmail** (Google)
- **Microsoft**
- **Okta**

### Enter your password

On the next screen, enter your password to complete sign-in.

</Steps>

## Forgot Password

If you've forgotten your password:

<Steps>

### Click Forgot Password

On the Login screen, click the **[Forgot Password]** link.

### Enter your registered email

Type your registered email address and submit the form. You will receive an email with a password reset link.

### Set your new password

Click the link in the email and set a new password. Once changed, you will receive a confirmation email.

</Steps>

## Account Lockout

AIDA enforces security measures for failed login attempts. Lockout settings are applied globally across all teams.

**Default values:**
- **Attempts:** 5 invalid attempts before lockout
- **Lockout duration:** 30 minutes
- **Minimum time between attempts:** 60 seconds

After exceeding the allowed attempts, your account will be locked and a message will be displayed. Wait for the lockout period to expire before trying again.

## Signing Up

New Team Owners can create an account directly from the Login screen.

<Steps>

### Start signup

Click the **Signup** button on the Login screen.

### Enter your details

Fill in your **Email**, **First name**, and **Last name**, then click **Create my account**.

### Set a password

Create a strong password and click **Next**.

### Set up your team

Enter your **Team name**, **Organization name**, and **Designation**, then click **Submit**.

</Steps>

After submitting, you will receive a confirmation email. Once approved, click the **Login** button in the email to access AIDA. The signed-up user is automatically granted the **Team Owner** role.

## Multiple Team Login

If you are a member of multiple teams, you will be directed to a **Team Selection** page after login. This page displays all your teams as cards showing the team name, ID, and a copy option.

- Click any team card to enter that team's workspace.
- Use the search field to find a team by name.

## Creating a New Team

Any registered AIDA user can create a new team.

<Steps>

### Go to team selection

After logging in, you will be directed to the Team Selection page.

### Click Create

Click the **Create** button on the Team Selection page.

### Enter team details

Enter the **Team name** and **Organization name**, then click **Submit**.

</Steps>

You will be automatically logged into the new team and redirected to the **Create Bot** page. You are the **Team Owner** of any team you create.

<Callout>
**Multi-team notes:**
1. Actions such as revoking, deactivating, and deleting users are team-specific.
2. Two-factor authentication (2FA) is not team-specific — it applies globally to your account.
3. When a session expires and you re-login, you will be directed back to the team you were last in.
</Callout>

## Logging Out

<Steps>

### Open the profile dropdown

Click the **profile icon** in the top-right header.

### Click Logout

Select **Logout** from the dropdown.

### Confirm logout

A confirmation prompt will appear warning that unsaved changes will be lost. Click **Confirm** to log out, or close the dialog to return to the app.

</Steps>
