# Multilingual

AIDA's multilingual feature translates user messages into the bot's base language for classification, and translates bot responses back into the user's language. This lets a single bot serve users across many languages without duplicating intents.

Multilingual is available to **Admin**, **Team Owner**, and **Manager** roles.

## Two toggles

| Toggle | Effect when ON |
|---|---|
| **User Query** | The user's message is translated into the bot's base language before being sent to the classifier |
| **Response** | The bot's reply is translated back into the user's language before being shown |

You can enable one, both, or neither — depending on whether you want the bot to accept input in other languages, respond in other languages, or both.

## Supported response types

Translation applies to all of these response types:

- Intent response
- Response with suggestions
- Response with parameters
- Response with gallery card
- Response with picture card
- DocBrain response
- LLM response
- Omega cards
- Blitzico response
- Blitzico conversation nodes
- AIDA flows

## LLM settings

Multilingual uses an LLM for translation. You can configure:

| Setting | Range | Default |
|---|---|---|
| **Model** | Choose from the dropdown | — |
| **Prompt** | Editable template | — |
| **Timeout** | 5 – 180 seconds | 30 |
| **Temperature** | 0 – 1 | 0.2 |

Lower temperature values produce more predictable, literal translations; higher values allow more creative phrasing at the risk of meaning drift. **0.2 is a safe default** for help-desk use.

## Enabling multilingual

<Steps>

### Go to Bot Settings

Click **Bot Settings** from the side menu.

### Open Multilingual

Click the Multilingual section.

### Turn on the toggle(s) you need

Enable **User Query** and/or **Response**.

### Confirm with OTP

An OTP is sent to the Team Owner's email. Enter it to confirm.

</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
  OTP confirmation is required every time you enable multilingual features. Make sure your Team Owner has email access before starting.
</Callout>
