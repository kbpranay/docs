## Bot Enable / Disable Toggle

Each integration channel has an enable/disable toggle that lets you quickly activate or deactivate the bot for that channel. Only **App Admin**, **Team Owner**, **Manager**, and **Admin** roles can manage bot status.

| Channel | Behavior when disabled |
|---|---|
| **Web/App (Chatbot)** | Chat window no longer loads for new sessions. Ongoing conversations remain active until the page is refreshed. |
| **WhatsApp** | Ongoing sessions continue and users can still send agent requests until session auto-closes. New messages receive an offline message. |
| **Voice** | Ongoing calls proceed as normal. New incoming calls are disconnected and receive an offline message. |

<Callout type="info">
  The offline message for Voice and WhatsApp is **editable**. Click outside the field after editing to auto-save.

  Interactions that occur while a channel is disabled are not included in training data — they are only counted under Total Conversations in Live Data and Analytics.
</Callout>
